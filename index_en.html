Ivity is being developed to help anyone change their lifestyle.

The idea is to create a free app that helps with demanding tasks and habit changes. We're still at the beginning of our journey, and we count on your help for suggestions and improvements.

The product you're receiving is an MVP - Minimum Viable Product, with only the basic features, but theoretically stable.

Pomodoro: To help complete complex tasks, we have a Pomodoro screen, an efficient way to make work/study less boring.

App Monitor: To keep track of how much time you spend on an application, the user selects a limit and receives a warning when they exceed the time.

Goals and Subtasks: Repetitive goals to be achieved, can be daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly, each with a weight.

Events: One-time, non-repeating goals like meetings, appointments, and more.

In the future, I plan to add more specific features, like an AI to help the user organize, and a notes block.
